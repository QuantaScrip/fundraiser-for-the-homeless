    FAQ

    Where does the money go?

    Funds are used to support people experiencing financial hardship (food, rent, utilities, medical, emergencies). A summary ledger and updates will be posted on this page.

    Is this tax-deductible?

    Donations to individuals are typically not tax-deductible. If you need a deductible receipt, donate via a registered nonprofit.

    What is the max donation?

    Single donations are capped at $10,000,000. The form prevents higher amounts.

    How do I verify transparency?

    We post regular updates and anonymized receipts. You can also join livestreams or view the public ledger spreadsheet.
